## Peering Policy

Freifunk Vechta (AS208733) maintains an open peering policy and peers
at mutual exchange points, subject to the requirements outlined below.

## Requirements

- A public ASN with at least one globally routable IPv6 prefix
- Up-to-date IRR entries
- Up-to-date PeeringDB entry
- Up-to-date RIR Whois information
- Compliance with industry best practices (e.g., BCP-38, prefix filtering).
- Accurate route-set or AS-SET and prefix maximums must be defined.
- Active NOC contact required for responsiveness.

## Filtering & Security

- We enforce strict IRR filtering and RPKI Route Origin Validation
- Filters are regenerated every 7 days
